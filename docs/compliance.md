# ESG & Compliance Framework

## Standards Followed

This platform follows:

- GHG Protocol
- ISO 14064
- Scope 1, 2, 3 Emissions

## Methodology

### Electricity
Emission = kWh × Regional Emission Factor

### Fuel
Emission = Liters × Fuel Factor

### Logistics
Emission = Distance × Weight × Carrier Factor

### Products
Emission = Material Footprint + Transport
